Information on horns for characters who have modified their horns in one way or another

Coil - There's a crystal on the left horn. It's cold. She put it there herself.
Hyperlaser - The antenna on his helmet are not his horns. His horns are stubs. He used to have horns.
Katana - Horns are naturally red, but dyed teal for the Church in his youth
Rocket - Horns technically have little branching out parts, but he carves them to look like Zuka's
Scythe - Last time I checked her horns are naturally black and are dyed greenish teal. For the Church
Shuriken - (Might get retconned later) base horns are pink, he paints them green
